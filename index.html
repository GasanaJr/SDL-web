Overview

Self-directed(S.D.L.) learning is a lesson offered in the first semester of the first year at the African Leadership University. S.D.L. is learning in which the conceptualization, design, conduct, and evaluation of a learning project are directed by the learner through 4 nodes: Readiness to learn, setting learning goals, engaging the learning process, and evaluating learning.

SDL encompasses many aspects of learning, such as academic writing, problem solving through various challenges, peer feedback, emotional intelligence, self-awareness, self-reflection..among others. Students are grouped into NegPods, where they get together and learn together, hence developing team work. In the 12 weeks of SDL, students learn, engage, get informed, and have fun doing all that.
